The Best Movies According to Kavivasanthan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1.Soorarai Pottru

Film synopsis:

Maara, a young man from a remote village, dreams of launching his own airline service.
However, he must overcome several obstacles and challenges in order to be successful in his quest.

2.Ratchasan

Film synopsis:

Arun gives up on his dream of becoming a filmmaker and takes up the job of a police officer after his father's death.
He then attempts to track down a psychotic killer who targets schoolgirls.

3.Jai Bhim

Film synopsis:

A pregnant woman from a primitive tribal community, searches desperately for her husband, who is missing from police custody.
A High Court advocate rises in support to find her husband and seek justice for them.
